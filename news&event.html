Upcoming News, Events, and Festivities in Oman

Local Celebrations

Oman National Day

Date: November 18, 2023

Oman National Day is celebrated with parades, fireworks, and cultural performances across the country. It commemorates the Sultan's ascension to the throne and showcases Omani pride.

Muscat Festival

Date: January 2024 (Exact dates TBA)

The Muscat Festival is a month-long celebration featuring cultural performances, food stalls, and entertainment for all ages. It highlights Oman's heritage and traditions.
