Transportation Services in Oman

Explore the various transportation options available in Oman, including buses, taxis, and ferries.

Buses

Oman has a well-established bus network that connects major cities and towns.

Routes

  • Muscat to Salalah
  • Muscat to Nizwa
  • Muscat to Sohar

Schedules

Route Departure Arrival Fare
Muscat to Salalah 8:00 AM 4:00 PM 10 OMR
Muscat to Nizwa 9:00 AM 11:00 AM 5 OMR
Muscat to Sohar 10:00 AM 12:00 PM 3 OMR

Booking Options

You can book your bus tickets online through Oman Tourism.

Interactive Map

Taxis

Taxis are widely available in Oman and are a convenient way to travel short distances.

Fares

The starting fare is 1 OMR, with an additional charge of 0.2 OMR per kilometer.

Booking Options

You can book taxis through apps like Careem and Uber.

Ferries

Ferries operate between Muscat and various islands, providing a scenic travel option.

Routes

  • Muscat to Khasab
  • Muscat to Masirah Island

Schedules

Route Departure Arrival Fare
Muscat to Khasab 7:00 AM 10:00 AM 15 OMR
Muscat to Masirah Island 1:00 PM 3:00 PM 20 OMR

Booking Options

Ferry tickets can be booked online through Oman Ferries.
